Ivan Mitevski

I am a Hess Postdoctoral Fellow at Princeton University working with Gabriel Vecchi. Prior to this, I got my PhD at Columbia University in August 2023 working with Lorenzo Polvani and Clara Orbe.

My research focuses on understanding how the climate system responds to a wide range of CO₂ perturbations. To achieve this, I conduct experiments employing climate models with varying levels of complexity, ranging from comprehensive Earth System Models to idealized conceptual models. My work spans climate sensitivity, radiative feedbacks, forcing, and large-scale atmospheric and ocean dynamics.

Feel free to contact me for discussions or potential collaborations.
